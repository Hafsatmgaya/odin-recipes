Chicken Tikka Masala with Naan

Description

Chicken tikka masala is a popular Indian-inspired dish made with tender pieces of chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. The chicken is usually marinated in yogurt and spices before being grilled or cooked, giving it a deep, smoky flavor that blends beautifully with the sauce.

This dish is often served with soft, warm naan bread, which is perfect for scooping up the flavorful sauce. The combination of creamy texture, bold spices, and tender chicken makes this meal a favorite around the world.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (boneless, cut into pieces)
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato puree
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Naan bread

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices.
  2. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes (or longer for better flavor).
  3. Cook or grill the chicken until slightly charred and set aside.
  4. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until soft.
  5. Add garlic and ginger, then cook briefly.
  6. Pour in tomato puree and cook until thickened.
  7. Add spices (garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, cumin).
  8. Stir in cream and mix well.
  9. Add the cooked chicken and simmer for a few minutes.
  10. Serve hot with naan bread.
