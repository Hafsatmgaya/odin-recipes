Chicken Tikka Masala with Naan

Description

Chicken tikka masala is a popular Indian-inspired dish made with tender pieces of chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. The chicken is usually marinated in yogurt and spices before being grilled or cooked, giving it a deep, smoky flavor that blends beautifully with the sauce.

This dish is often served with soft, warm naan bread, which is perfect for scooping up the flavorful sauce. The combination of creamy texture, bold spices, and tender chicken makes this meal a favorite around the world.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (boneless, cut into pieces)
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato puree
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Naan bread
