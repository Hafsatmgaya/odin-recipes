Stir-Fry Noodles with Chicken

Description

Stir-fry noodles with chicken is a quick and flavorful dish made by tossing noodles with vegetables, chicken, and a savory sauce. It is cooked over high heat, which gives the dish a slightly smoky taste and keeps the vegetables crisp and fresh.

This dish is perfect for a quick meal, combining soft noodles with juicy chicken and crunchy vegetables. The sauce, made with soy sauce and seasonings, ties everything together into a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Noodles (spaghetti or egg noodles)
  • Chicken (sliced)
  • Carrots (julienned)
  • Bell peppers
  • Cabbage
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Ginger
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken and cook until tender, then set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, then sauté briefly.
  5. Add onions and vegetables (carrots, peppers, cabbage) and stir-fry.
  6. Add the cooked chicken and mix well.
  7. Pour in soy sauce and seasoning.
  8. Add the cooked noodles and toss everything together.
  9. Stir-fry for a few minutes until well combined.
  10. Serve hot.
